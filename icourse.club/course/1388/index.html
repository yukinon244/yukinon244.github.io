简介

 002001: Solid State Physics A

  • Basic Information: This is an undergraduate level course for those whose major is physics. It is designed to help those who plan to be experimentalists to grasp the basic fundamental concepts and models in condensed matter physics so that they would find themselves easier in reading literature. It is also designed to facilitate those who plan to be theoreticians with a broader view of the condensed matter physics. 
    The background needed is Quantum Mechanics. More Information about this course is here.
